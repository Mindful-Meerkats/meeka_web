About

Mindful Meerkats is an open, not-only-for-profit collective of people who create products around changing behaviour by applying methods from Behavioural Economics, Social and Gameful Design.
Our modern times are characterised by a multitude of complex and complicated challenges. The way how we have lived since the advent of agriculture has to change dramatically for us to continue our high standards of living, whilst giving other cultures the same opportunities.
We love the planet, our home. And we love people, our tribe. It is our vision to help both by spreading ideas that help people who arguably have all they need to be happier with less. To live outwardly simple but inwardly rich, with a strong connection to people and environment. Our primary focus at the moment is “Meeka”. Meeka is a game that invites players with challenges, called Quests , to transform their daily lives towards aware living. In doing Quests , we hope that people experience the essence of a happy life.
Other ideas we want to explore in the future are a podcast series, seminars, and workshops.
As of now, we fund our work via donations. If you, dear reader, are interested to support our work and get to know more about us, please get in touch with us.
