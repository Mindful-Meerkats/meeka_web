About

"Meeka" is your playful lifestyle companion.

Everyone has ambitions and goals in life. And for most, happiness and life satisfaction is the cornerstone of these goals. However, in the speed of daily, modern life, it is not easy to match your shortterm tasks with your longterm goals. Wanting to contribute to a better world is not easy when one has to meet everyday needs. And that is especially true, when there is awareness of the complexity of areas where one could contribute. Meeka helps by suggesting one little, impactful thing.

Change yourself. Change the world!
